### Steps a DevOps should take
Connect to LLMs (APIs, endpoints)
Expose systems as tools for AI agents -Tools integration
Understand and Design multi-step AI workflows - Agents workflow and Architecture
Connect AI to your data - Data Access (RAG)
Deploy AI services at scale - Infrastructure for AI(k8s/Docker)
Protect data, control access -Security and Governance
Monitor Ai decision and behaviour - Observability
Scale AI services for prod - Automation and Scaling
Build the bridge between AI and systems with MCP
