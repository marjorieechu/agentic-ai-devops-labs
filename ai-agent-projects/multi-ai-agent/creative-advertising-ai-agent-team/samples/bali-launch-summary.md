# Bali Launch Sample

Prompt:

`Launch campaign for a new eco-friendly water bottle in Bali`

Selected campaigns:

- `Refill the Island`
- `Hydrate Like a Local`

Why they won:

- `Refill the Island` gives the launch a tangible utility layer through refill stations, which makes the sustainability story visible and shareable.
- `Hydrate Like a Local` fits Bali's wellness, surf, and tourism behaviors, which makes partnerships and influencer execution more practical.

Channel highlights:

- `twitter`: refill map drops, creator challenges, and user-generated landmark check-ins
- `linkedin`: sustainability partnership narrative and destination-marketing case study angle
- `email`: refill-guide launch sequence and traveler nurture flow
- `short_video`: day-in-the-life Bali routes and creator footage across wellness and surf settings
